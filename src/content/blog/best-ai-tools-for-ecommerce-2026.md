## 1. Jasper — Best for Ad Copy & Marketing Content

If you're running META or Google ads for your store, Jasper is a no-brainer.

**What impressed us:** We A/B tested Jasper-generated ad copy against our best-performing human-written ads on a client's META campaigns. The result? Jasper's copy achieved a **23% higher CTR** and comparable ROAS.

**Best features:**
- Brand voice training (feed it your existing copy, it learns your tone)
- Campaign workflow (generates headlines, descriptions, and CTA variations in one go)
- Integration with Surfer SEO for content optimization

**Where it falls short:**
- Expensive for solo operators
- Sometimes generates generic "marketing speak" — you need to edit

**Pricing:** Starts at $49/month for Creator plan, $125/month for Pro.

**Verdict:** Worth every penny if you spend $1,000+/month on ads. The time saved alone pays for the subscription.

---

## 2. ChatGPT Plus — Best All-Around Content Tool

You probably already use ChatGPT. But are you using it *effectively* for e-commerce?

**What impressed us:** With the right prompts, ChatGPT Plus generates product descriptions that convert better than most junior copywriters. We developed a prompt framework specifically for e-commerce that consistently produces high-quality output.

**Best use cases for e-commerce:**
- Product descriptions (features → benefits → objection handling)
- Customer email templates
- FAQ generation from product specs
- Competitor analysis summaries
- Social media content calendars

**Where it falls short:**
- No brand voice memory across sessions (without custom GPTs)
- Can hallucinate product features — always fact-check

**Pricing:** $20/month for Plus. Best value in AI right now.

**Verdict:** The Swiss Army knife. Not the best at any single task, but good enough at everything to be indispensable.

## 3. Midjourney — Best for Product Imagery

Stock photos are dead. Midjourney generates lifestyle product imagery that actually sells.

**What impressed us:** A client selling home decor used Midjourney to create lifestyle scenes for their products. Their product pages with AI-generated lifestyle images saw a **34% increase in add-to-cart rate** compared to plain product photos.

**Best use cases:**
- Lifestyle product photography
- Ad creative backgrounds
- Social media content
- Brand mood boards

**Where it falls short:**
- Can't perfectly replicate your exact product (yet)
- Requires skill in prompt engineering

**Pricing:** $10/month for Basic, $30/month for Standard.

**Verdict:** Essential for any store that can't afford a full product photography setup.

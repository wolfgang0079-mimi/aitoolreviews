## FAQ

### What is the best free AI video generator?

Pika offers the most generous free tier for text-to-video generation. Kling also offers daily free credits. For talking-head videos, HeyGen has a limited free tier.

### Can AI-generated video be used commercially?

Yes, most tools grant commercial usage rights on paid plans. Always check the specific terms of service â€” some tools have restrictions on certain content types or require attribution.

### How long can AI-generated videos be?

Most tools generate clips of 5-15 seconds per generation. Sora supports up to 60 seconds on the Pro plan. For longer videos, you'll typically need to combine multiple clips in a video editor.

### Do I need a powerful computer for AI video generation?

No. All seven tools on this list are cloud-based. You generate videos through a web browser, and the AI processing happens on the company's servers. Any modern computer with internet access works.

### Which AI video tool has the best quality?

Runway Gen-4 and Sora produce the highest quality output in our testing. Veo 2.5 is close behind. Quality differences are narrowing rapidly across all tools.

### Can AI video replace real videography?

For certain use cases (social media content, quick ads, concept visualization), yes. For professional productions, brand campaigns, and content requiring specific actors or locations, real videography remains superior. AI video is best used as a complement to traditional production, not a replacement.
